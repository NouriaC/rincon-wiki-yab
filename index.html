Yann Arthus-Bertrand

 Photography Filmography Envrionmentalism

Yann Arthus-Bertrand (French, b.1946) is a photographer and journalist. Specializing in documentaries on sports, wildlife, and arial photography, he has published work in Paris Match, GEO, and others.

In 1991 he founded Altitude Agency, the first photo agency specializing in arial photography. As part of his environmental activism, in 2012 Arthus-Betrand founded Hope, a non-profit production company of documentaries. His best-known film Home is composed almost entirely of arial shots of the Earth, and depicts man-made threats to the environment. Arthus-Betrand is the recipient of numerous awards.
